What is KROK

A space where
every voice matters

KROK — meaning "step" — is built for Belarusians who believe in the future of their country. It is an independent digital platform designed from the ground up to protect its users from surveillance, data collection, and political pressure.

Unlike existing platforms captured by regime-aligned actors or compromised opposition media, KROK operates with full editorial and technical independence. No backdoors. No cooperation with state security services. No exceptions.

🔒

Private by Design

No IP logging. No metadata collection. No third-party tracking. User anonymity is non-negotiable.

🗣️

Free Expression

A moderated but open space for civic discussion, news sharing, and community organisation.

🛡️

Independent

Not affiliated with any political party, opposition group, or external government. Fully independent.

🌍

Diaspora-Inclusive

Built for Belarusians wherever they are — inside the country or in exile across Europe and beyond.

Why it matters

Belarus needs an
independent voice

Since 2020, civic space in Belarus has been systematically destroyed. Independent media is banned. Journalists are imprisoned. Online platforms are monitored and used to identify and persecute dissidents.

At the same time, many diaspora-run media outlets have been captured by politically motivated actors who suppress independent voices under the guise of opposition activity.

KROK exists to fill that gap — a platform built on principles, not politics. A step toward a future where every Belarusian can speak freely.
